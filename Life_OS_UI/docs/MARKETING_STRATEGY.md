## üéß Part 2: DJ World Service Expansion

### 1. The "Bar-to-Bar" Foundation (Current)

- **Strategy:** Maintain high-touch personal relationships.
- **Expansion:** Use the "Enterprise" tier of Life.io to show Bar Managers how you can help them manage _other_ DJs. This turns you from a "vendor" into a "consultant."

### 2. The Wedding Pivot (High Trust)

- **Brand Shift:** Focus on "The Story." Use video testimonials from couples.
- **Lead Magnet:** "The Ultimate 2025 Wedding Music Checklist" - captures emails of engaged couples.
- **Platforms:** The Knot, WeddingWire (only if ROI is proven), and localized Instagram ads targeted at "Recently Engaged" statuses.

### 3. The Corporate Pivot (High Reliability)

- **Brand Shift:** Focus on "The Production Partner." Emphasize A/V, lighting, and timeline management.
- **Targeting:** LinkedIn Outreach to local "HR Managers" and "Office Coordinators."
- **Hook:** "Stress-Free Corporate Events: Professional Sound & Lighting with a Vibe that Matches Your Brand."
