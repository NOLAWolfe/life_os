## 2. The Strategy: "The Hottest Dollar" (Attention)

We face a "Subscriber Floor" on all platforms before monetization. To bridge this gap, we need a **Volume & Consistency** strategy using "Starter Accounts".

### Phase 1: The Starter Accounts (The Grind)

- **Goal:** Reach monetization thresholds (e.g., 1000 subs/4000 hours on YT).
- **Method:** Automated/Semi-Automated Content Scheduling.
- **Metric:** Consistency (Daily Shorts/Reels, Weekly Long-form).
- **Style:** Lower production friction, high replay value.

### Phase 2: The Transition (The Brand)

- **Goal:** Convert "Viral" traffic into "Loyal" followers.
- **Method:** Introduce the "Life.io" personality. "How I built this automation," "My DJ workflow."
- **Metric:** Community Engagement (Comments, Discord joins).
